Who we are

Anne Marie Mahon

Anne Marie is an ecologist and environmental scientist with a passion to help reduce human impact on our natural world.

Inspired by the loss of biodiversity in coastal habitats, evident during her years as a marine biologist, she is now based in Portumna, Co. Galway, and works on R&D projects, biodiversity projects and outreach programmes to communicate more sustainable ways of living.

Leveraging her strong research background and knowledge of species and habitats, she shares her ideas through community engagement by facilitating workshops and projects.

Anne Marie is well-published in scientific literature and has been interviewed by and written for a wide range of media outlets. She has also been an invited speaker at numerous events pertaining to our relationship with plastics and ways of driving consumer-led change in our everyday lives.

Eileen Elli Ronayne

Eileen is a biologist committed to sustainability and nature conservation. Her work is driven by a strong belief that the solution to today’s most pressing environmental challenges involves incorporating sustainability into mainstream cultures, through top-down and bottom-up change, and redefining our relationship with nature.

She currently works as a coordinator for biodiversity and sustainability projects and outreach programmes – which day in and day out entails carrying out desktop research, creating media for a diverse range of audiences, participating as a speaker in nature tours and contributing to ecology surveys with her computer and field skills.

Because of her broad academic training and international background, Eileen brings to the table a multidimensional and systemic perspective – she grasps the details, sees the bigger picture and draws relevant connections to activate pragmatic and value-based solutions.

When she is not working, she’s most likely volunteering in her local bats’ group, nursing baby bats at home, or enabling sustainability in her daily life – whether it is learning to grow and forage food, formulate cosmetics or reduce her consumption footprint.
